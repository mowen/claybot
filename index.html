    Clay Davis Bot

    A Twitter bot inspired by the bots of Colin Mitchell. If you mention "clay davis" in a tweet, you will receive a "Shiiieeet!" reply.

    If you have triggered a reply from Clay Davis Bot, you won't receive another reply for the next hour. This is so that the bot is less likely to be reported as spam.

    Contacting the owner

    This bot was created by Martin Owen (@martinowen). If you're offended by it, please let him know rather than reporting it to Twitter. This kind of bot will be banned if too many people are unhappy with it (see The Current State of Bots on Twitter).
